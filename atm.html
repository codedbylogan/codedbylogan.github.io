Reflection of ATM

The ATM program has had a drastic impact on my regular life and school life. This program has taught me to type and understand the computer even more. Without being in ATM, I wouldn’t have been able to type up my essays for other classes in a quick amount of time. Learning about the more popular programs on the computer, like Google Docs, have really helped me with other classes and get my work done. We get to explore the world of computers which will help us later in life since a lot of it is technology based. I don’t write my essays with a pen and paper, I am instructed to type it on the computer. Without having this knowledge of the computer, I would be struggling with assignments, especially since this is the generation of technology. If I didn’t have Ms. McClure and Ms. Milton’s help, I would be having a difficult time with school.
